SEEING CALIPHS IN DREAMS

Hazrat Abubakar Siddeeqi . The first caliph was Hazrat Abubakar Siddeeqi i . Hazrat Ibn Sirin r said that if a person sees the first caliph in his dream; it indicates happiness and miracles. Hazrat Karmani r said that if a person dreams of Hazrat Abubakr i happy and alive in a city; it means that the people of that city give charity and Zakat in abundance. When the caliph is seen sad, it would be otherwise.

Hazrat Umar Bin Khattabi . The second caliph was Hazrat Umar Bin Khattabi i. Hazrat Ibn Sirin r said that if a person dreams of Hazrat Umar Bin Khattabi; it means that the person is fair and just. If the caliph is seen smiling and happy in a city, it means that justice and fairness will be visible in that city.

Hazrat Usman Bin Affani . The third caliph was Hazrat Usman Bin Affani. Hazrat Ibn Sirinr said that if a person dreams of Hazrat Usman Bin Affani laughing and in a happy mood, it means that the person will attain courage and miracles. If the caliph is seen cheerful and happy in a city, it means that the people of that city will be interested in learning the Holy Quran and gaining knowledge.

Hazrat Ali Murtazai . The fourth caliph was Hazrat Ali Murtazai. Hazrat Ibn Sirinr said that if a person dreams of Hazrat Ali Murtazai laughing and in a happy mood, it means that the person will attain courage, leadership and miracles. City in which the caliph is seen, will be characterised with justice and fairness; but there will sometimes be disputes and quarrels between them.

Dreaming of Remaining Companionsi . Hazrat Ibn Sirinr said that if a person dreams of the Prophets’g Companionsi, it means that the person will be alone in the way of Islam. He will be famous amongst Muslims as a truthful and having integrity.

Hazrat Imam Hassani & Hassaini . A person who dreams of Hazrat Imam Hassan i & Hassaini will achieve well-being, comfort and the status of martyrdom.

Hazrat Jaffar Tayyari . A person who dreams of Hazrat Jaffar Tayyari , will perform Hajj and go for Jihad.

Hazrat Abu Hurairahi & Ans Bin Maliki . If a person dreams of Hazrat Abu Hurairahi & Ans Bin Maliki, it means that the person will be inclined towards Sunnah and will like Shariat.

Hazrat Salman Farsii . If a person dreams of Hazrat Salman Farsii, Allahd will grant him knowledge of Quran and his Hereafter will be commendable.

Hazrat Abdullah Bin Abbasi & Ibn Masoodi . If a person dreams of Hazrat Abdullah Bin Abbasi or Ibn Masoodi, it means that the person will be a Mujahid.

Hazrat Bilal Habshii . If a person dreams of Hazrat Bilal Habshii, it means that he will become famous and will be rewarded for a person who calls to the daily prayer (Mua’zin). He will follow Sunnah.

Dreaming of Scholars, Sages & Ascetics

If a person dreams of scholars, sages and ascetics, it means that he will be enabled to do good deeds and gain knowledge. If a person dreams of a group of scholars and rulers gathering in a city or neighbourhood, it means that the people of that city will get help from ignorant people by speech, character, command and prohibitions.
