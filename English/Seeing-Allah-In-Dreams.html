Seeing Allah In Dream

Hazrat Daniyal (RA) said that a believer who sees Allah تعالیٰ in his dream indicates that he will see Allah تعالیٰ and his needs will be fulfilled. If the dreamer is standing and Allah تعالیٰ sees him; it is a sign of he being corrected, achieve control of inner self and his forgiveness will be ensured. If the dreamer is a sinner, he will repent. Allah تعالیٰ says in the Holy Quran.

وَقَرَّبْنَاہُ نَجِیًّا
"AND brought him near, confiding (to him).”
(Maryam 52/19)

وَمَاکَانَ لِبَشَرٍ اَنْ یُّکَلِّمَہُ اللّٰہُ اِلَّا وَحْیاً اَومِنْ وَّرَا ءِ حِجَاب
"and it is not for any human being that Allah should speak to him except by revelation or from behind a partition.”
(Ash Shura 51/42)

If a person dreams that Allah تعالیٰ has made him close, dear and respected, it is an indication of his forgiveness and he will see trouble in the world. If a person dreams that Allah تعالیٰ is advising him, it indicates that Allah’s تعالیٰw ill is not in it. Allah تعالیٰ says in the Holy Quran.

یَعِظُکُمْ لَعَلَّکُمْ تَذَ کَّرُ وْ نَ
"He admonishes you that perhaps you will be reminded.”
(An Nahl 90/16)

If a person dreams that Allah تعالیٰ is giving him good news, it shows His تعالیٰ pleasure and on the contrary; if a person sees that Allah تعالیٰ scaring / frightening him, it indicates His تعالیٰ anger. If a person dreams that he is standing with his head bowed before Allah تعالیٰ, it indicates grief and sorrow. Allah تعالیٰ says in the Holy Quran.

وَلَوْ ترٰٓی اِذِ الٗمُجٗرِمُوْنَ نَا کِسُوْ ارُ ء ُوْ سِھِمْ عِنْدَ رَ بِّھِمْ
"If you could but see when the criminals are bowing their heads before their lord .”
(As Sajdah 12/32)

Hazrat Kirmani (RA) said that if a person dreams of Allah تعالیٰ giving him something; it is a sign of calamity and trouble in this world. If a person dreams that Allah تعالیٰ sees him with pleasure/ happiness, it indicates that Allah تعالیٰ will grant him paradise and His تعالیٰ sight. If a person dreams that he has been given something from the goods of the world, it is a sign of serious illness, but he will be rewarded for it. Some interpreters argue that the person will be punished. If a person dreams of seeing Allah تعالیٰ and a third person says that He تعالیٰ is Allah تعالیٰ; it is an indication of Kingship for the dreamer. If a person dreams of Allah تعالیٰ coming to some place in this word, it indicates that the people of that area are oppressed and Allah تعالیٰ will help those people; if there is famine and hardship, then there will be generosity and if the inhabitants of the area are sinners, they will repent. If a person dreams of Allah تعالیٰ coming to his house and caresses his head or puts on a robe, it is a sign of Allah’s grace; but this person will bear sorrow and illness in this world. If a person sees Allah’s تعالیٰ divine face in a dream, which cannot be expressed, it is an indication of grief and sorrow. If a person dreams that Allah تعالیٰ has given him another name, it indicates honor and greatness. If Allah تعالیٰ has dominated him, it is a sign of asceticism and piety. If a person dreams that Allah تعالیٰ has brought him close to Himself تعالیٰ, it indicates that the person's death is near.

Hazrat Jabir al-Maghribi (RA) said that if a person dreams of Allah تعالیٰ in a city or a village, it indicates that the good people of that place will get respect, honour and position. There will be no more oppression at that place. Allah تعالیٰ say in the Holy Quran.

اَللّٰہُ یَحْکُمُ بَیْنَھَمْ یَوْمَ الْقِیٰمَتِہ ِ فِیْمَا کَا نُوْا فِیْہِ یَخْتَلِفُوْنَ
"Allah will judge between them on the day of resurrection concerning that over which they used to differ.”
(Al Baqarah 113/2)

If a person dreams that Allah تعالیٰ is angry over a city, it indicates that either judge of the city is unjust, ruler of the city is oppressing his subjects or the learned people of this city are irreligious. If a thief sees this dream, his hands and feet will be cut off and there will be trial, destruction and murder in the city. Allah تعالیٰ says in the Holy Quran.

وَھُوَ الْقَا ھِرُ فَوْ قَ عِبَا دِہٖ ویُرْ سِلُ عَلَیْکُمْ حَفَظَتہ ً حَتّٰی اِذَا جَاۤ ءَ اَحَدَ کُمُ الْمَوْ تُ تَوَ فَّتْہُ رُسُلُنَا وَ ھُمْ لَا یُفَرِّطُوْنَ
"and he is the subjugator over his servants, and he sends over you guardian angels until, when death comes to one of you, our messengers take him, and they do not fail (in their duties).”
(Al Anam 61/6)

Hazrat Abdullah bin Abbas (RA) said, that if a person sees Allah تعالیٰ in a dream, he will remain in peace from fear; and if the person is a Muslim, he will see Allah تعالیٰ in the Hereafter. Allah تعالیٰ says in the Holy Quran.

لِلَّذِ یْنَ اَحْسَنُو ا الْحُسْنٰی و زِیَا دَ ۃُ ُ
"For them who have done good is the best (reward) and extra.”
(Yunus 26/10)

Dream interpreters say that if a person sees Allah تعالیٰ in a form that has never been seen before, it is a sign of His تعالیٰ grace and mercy. If the dreamer sees Allah تعالیٰ in the form of a person than that particular person will be dominant and renowned. If a person sees Allah تعالیٰ during prayer or Tasbeeh, it is a sign of His تعالیٰ mercy and forgiveness. If victorious fighters sees Allah تعالیٰ during battle, it is a sign of their forgiveness. If a person abuses Allah تعالیٰ in a dream, it is a sign of his disbelief. If a person dreams of Allah sitting on a throne or sleeping; these are inappropriate attributes and is an indication that the person will be a non-believer and sinner.

Hazrat Jafar Sadiq (RA) said that when Allah تعالیٰ is seen mercifully in a dream, it is due to the under mentioned seven reasons and if He تعالیٰ is seen in anger, the reasons would be opposite:-

1. Forgiveness
2.Peace from calamity and distress
3.Light, guidance and strength in religion
4.Victory over the oppressors
5.Peace from calamity and punishment of the Hereafter
6.The population and ruler of the country will be just
7.Honour and high status in this world and the Hereafter

If a judge dreams of Allah تعالیٰ looking at him with anger, it is a sign that he transgresses the Sharia and does compensate the oppressed; if a king / ruler dreams of Allah تعالیٰ looking at him with anger, it is a sign of him being cruel and unjust and if a scholar dreams of Allah تعالیٰ looking at him with anger, there is a disturbance in his religion and belief. In short, when seen with mercy, it indicates forgiveness and when seen with anger, it is a sign of punishment.
