Seeing Angels In Dream

If a king sees angels in a dream, it is testimony of his piety and honesty and if a scholar seeing angels in dream is testimony of his piousness. Amongst all the angels the u/m are revered.

Hazrat Jibrail (AS). Hazrat Ibn Sirin (RA) said that if a person sees Hazrat Jibrail (AS) cheerful and generous; it indicates that the person will win over his enemy and achieve his goal and on the contrary it indicates sorrow and grief. If a person sees in his dream that Hazrat Jibrail (AS) gives him good news or has prevented from doing something; it indicates honour, wealth and optimism. If Hazrat Jibrail (AS) gives the person something; he will be bestowed with a higher rank by the king. A few dream interpreters say, that if a person dreams of Hazrat Jibrail (AS) giving him something, it a sign of fear and terror. If a person tells the dreamer in his dream that this is Hazrat Jibrail (AS), it indicates that the person will be able to comprehend, achieve his wishes and will be known for his trustworthiness and generosity.

Hazrat Mikael (AS). If a person sees Hazrat Mikael (AS) in his dream, it is indicative of good news; as Hazrat Jibrail (AS) is bringer of dreams and Hazrat Mikael (AS) is bringer of glad tidings and blessings. Hazrat Ibn Sirin (RA) said that if a person dreams of Hazrat Mikael (AS) given him something, it is indicative of goodness and greatness. If a person dream of people calling him Mikael, it indicates that people will get good news and tidings from the dreamer and he will find great status.

Hazrat Israfeel (AS). Hazrat Ibn Sirin r said that if a person dreams of Hazrat Israfeel (AS), it is indicative of strength, greatness, majesty, knowledge and wisdom. The country where Hazrat Israfeel (AS) is seen indicates peace and prosperity for the land. If Hazrat Israfeel (AS) is seen blowing trumpet it indicates calamity and enticement. If however, a trumpet is seen with Hazrat Israfeel (AS) but he doesn’t blow; it means that there will be calamity, enticement and great fear in the city. If a person dreams of Hazrat Israfeel (AS) in his home and the dreamer awakes from sleep, it indicates that the city head will be angry with him. Allah says in the Holy Quran.

وَنُفِخَ فِی الصُّورِ فَصَعِقَ مَنْ فِی السّمٰٰوٰتِ وَمَنْ فِی الْاَرْضِ
“And the Horn will be blown, and whoever is in the heavens and whoever is on the earth will fall dead.”
(Az Zumar 39/68)
